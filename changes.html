Student --> Financial Aid --> Financial Aid Portal OLD

Access the MPCC Financial Aid Portal

You can:

  • View a list of documents we've received from you and a list of the documents still outstanding 
  • Complete required documents
  • View and accept your financial aid awards 
  • View your student loan history
  • Check the status of your student loans for the current year

If you are a new user of the MPCC Financial Aid Portal, click on First Time User (below the Submit button) and follow the prompts. Your SSN will be all nine digits, no spaces or dashes. You must have a financial aid record already established at Mid-Plains Community College in order to use this system. You can complete a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) using our school code 002557. The Financial Aid office will contact you when your Financial Aid Portal is available.

Contact the Financial Aid office for assistance.
