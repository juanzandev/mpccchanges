<p>Mid-Plains Community College offers several student worker opportunities. Students who are eligible for and have indicated an interest in Federal Work Study will be given preference. For students not eligible for Federal Work Study, positions may be funded through the institution's Student Clerical budget; however, the Student Clerical budget is very limited. Federal Work Study is also limited. Placement in all positions is subject to availability of funds.</p>
